# LexGuard AI 🛡️
### An Intelligent Cross-Border Legal & Industrial Document Auditor

LexGuard AI is a universal legal intelligence platform that simplifies, translates, and audits any formal document — from local rental agreements to complex international trade treaties.

## What it does
- 📄 Audits legal documents and gives a **Legal Health Score (1-100)**
- 🔴🟡🟢 Highlights clauses as Safe, Neutral, or Critical
- 🌐 Cross-border compliance check (India vs USA/Germany etc.)
- 🗣️ Multilingual summaries (English, Hindi, Punjabi)
- ⚖️ Provides counter-drafts for predatory clauses

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, Socket.io
- **AI Engine:** Python, FastAPI, LangChain, Gemini AI
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas
